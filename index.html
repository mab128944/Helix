About Helix Agro Solutions

Founded in Kasur, Punjab, with a mission to modernize Pakistani agriculture.

Our Vision

To lead Pakistan's agricultural transition toward science-based, sustainable feeding solutions that ensure food security and maximize farmer profitability.

Our Mission

To provide 100% natural, highly nutritious, and perfectly fermented corn silage directly to livestock owners, bypassing intermediaries to ensure fair pricing and premium quality.

The Science Meets Nature

Our logo—a combination of the DNA helix and a green leaf—represents our core philosophy. We rely on rigorous agricultural science (testing pH, dry matter, and fermentation metrics) to deliver a product that is entirely natural.

Our Product

Premium Corn Silage

Corn silage is a high-energy forage crop utilized globally for dairy and beef cattle. We harvest our corn at the exact milk-line stage to ensure the perfect starch-to-fiber ratio, followed by anaerobic fermentation.
